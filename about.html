Ben&Ben History

Formed in 2016 by Paolo and Miguel Benjamin Guico, the duo first gained recognition after winning 2nd runner-up in the PhilPop songwriting competition with their debut single, "Tinatangi".

Due to their growing success, the duo expanded the lineup to include percussion, violins, keyboards, bass, and drums.

The nine-piece band settled on the name "Ben&Ben" as they transitioned into a major musical force in the Philippines.
